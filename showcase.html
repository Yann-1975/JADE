Project 3 — Problem-Solving Page (Design Concept)

Role: Designer & developer of the demo solution.

Problem: Demonstrate web development skills applied to a restaurant's digital transformation.

Design concept & rationale

  • Clarity: Hierarchy prioritises contact details, social links and booking form.
  • Practicality: Email generation via mailto: provides a simple MVP without a backend.
  • Mobile-first: Layout optimised for phones and tablets.
  • Accessibility: Form labels and progressive enhancement included.
