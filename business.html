3) Restaurant Digital Transformation (Problem-Solving Page)

Role: Project Architect & Lead

Problem: A traditional restaurant needed to improve customer engagement, streamline bookings and modernize communication channels.

Solution & Outcome: Designed and implemented a responsive web experience (see the Problem-Solving page) including social media integration, contact details and an interactive booking/feedback form that generates emails for staff. Outcome: modern UX, faster booking flow, and integrated social presence.

Design concept behind the Problem-Solving page

The Problem-Solving page was structured to satisfy the business needs: quick access to contact, integrated social proof, and a booking flow that requires no backend (uses mailto pre-filled emails). This provides a low-cost, fast-to-deploy solution for small hospitality businesses while enabling easy analytics tracking and progressive enhancement into a full booking system later.
