Business Showcase

Project 1 – Restaurant Digital Transformation (Problem-Solving Page)

Role: CTO / Web Developer

Problem: The restaurant needed a digital solution to improve customer engagement, streamline bookings, and modernise communication.

Outcome: I designed a structured web-based solution featuring integrated social media, contact details, and an email-generating interactive form. The design focuses on simplicity, customer experience, and supporting the restaurant’s transformation goals.

Design Concept Explanation

The “Problem-Solving Page” is intentionally simple, accessible, and efficient. Its structure includes:

  • A business context introduction outlining goals.
  • Social media integration for improved engagement.
  • Contact information for transparency and trust.
  • An interactive form that generates email requests without a database.

This structure supports smooth user interaction while aligning with the restaurant’s digital transformation needs.
