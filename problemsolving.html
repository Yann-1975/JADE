Restaurant Digital Transformation — Case Study

Business Context

Small restaurant seeking to modernize customer engagement, streamline bookings and improve communication without heavy upfront investment. Goals: better online presence, social integration, easy bookings, and analytics.

Key Requirements implemented

  • Social media integration (Facebook / Instagram / Twitter)
  • Clear display of contact details
  • Interactive booking/feedback form that generates an email for staff
  • Responsive design and accessible UI

How the web solution works

The page provides a simple booking/feedback form. When a customer fills the form and clicks Send Booking the site automatically opens the visitor's email client with a pre-filled email to the restaurant address. This approach requires no server and is ideal for fast deployment. It also allows staff to receive bookings directly in their inbox and respond using normal email workflows.

Booking / Feedback Form

Why this design?

The form-first layout puts the user's action (booking) front and centre while showing contact and social proof. Using a mailto-based booking avoids the need for a backend yet still provides staff a clear structured email with all booking details. The page is responsive, accessible, and designed so the form can be migrated to a database-backed booking engine later with minimal changes.
